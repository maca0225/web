Becoming The Pickle.

The story began when I got my first job at Target when I was 16. During orientation I thought we were suppose to put nicknames on our badges such as Nighthalwk or Strawberry Shortcake, so I thought what better name to do than the very best...PICKLE. Since that day everyone believed my real name was pickle (besides HR of course) and I got so tired of correcting the customers, so I finally went along with it. Yes maam my name is Pickle, yes sir it is European... Its actually pronounced PEECKEEL.
